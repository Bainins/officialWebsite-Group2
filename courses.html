Dr. Alimatu-Saadia Yussif has dedicated her career to impactful research that advances knowledge in her field.
Her work explores critical issues, offering innovative insights and solutions that contribute to academic discourse and real-world applications.
Through her research, she has published in reputable journals, collaborated with experts, and participated in conferences that shape the future of her discipline.

Her reasearch activities include:

  • E-learning Related Topics
  • Intelligent tutoring systems
  • Mobile and Ubiquitous Systems
  • Transcript analysis of users collaborative learning experience
  • Internet of things (IoTs)
  • Science Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) Education and System Development
  • Semantic Web Applications



Beyond her research, Dr. Yussif is an active member of several professional bodies, where she engages with peers, shares expertise, and contributes to the growth of her profession.
These affiliations provide a platform for networking, mentorship, and continuous learning, reinforcing her commitment to excellence and development in her field.

She is a member of the following professional bodies:

  • University Teachers Association of Ghana (UTAG) 2007
  • Ghana Science Association (GSA) 2009
  • Information Technologist Association of Ghana (ITAG)
  • Internet Society Ghana (ISOC)
  • African Network Operators Group (AFNOG)
  • Women in Information Technology (WoTI)
  • African Women in Computing (Afchix)
  • Women TechMakers
  • Women in Science Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), UCC
